R & D overview


We have dedicated R&D centres for Active pharmaceutical ingredients (API’s) and Finished dosage forms. Our R&D activities are aimed at delivering cost effective therapeutically efficient products that improve the quality of millions of people’s lives across the globe. Our strong R&D skills along with understanding of the local regulatory requirements have enabled successful registrations of products in India as well as worldwide markets.

Regulatory compliance
We ensure that our development work is in line with the current Regulatory requirements for various markets. We consider the compliance as our top most responsibility.
Innovations
We believe and always work towards innovative, efficient, cost effective and quality product development. We are constantly looking after process improvements to provide best solutions to our partners.
Trust and integrity
For any successful relationship, Trust and integrity are most important parameters and we always try our best to uphold these values to become a reliable partner.
Quality
We are committed to deliver robust and quality products which will meet the regulatory requirements as well as our customer expectations. We work hard for improving the process to meet the quality expectations.
