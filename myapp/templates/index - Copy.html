About Exemed Pharmaceuticals

Exemed is a regulatory-compliant, customer-centric, and global leading pharmaceutical company in India.

Since

2009

It was established in 2009 as part of a venture initiated by Mr. Pranav Patel. It is supported by two manufacturing facilities involved in the manufacturing of Drug-intermediates and specialty chemicals. Our state-of-the-art US FDA, EDQM, EU GMP certified facility is located in Luna Vadodara and Vapi and headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat..
