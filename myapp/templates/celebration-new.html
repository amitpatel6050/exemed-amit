Celebrations

We celebrate every success and different festivities to build momentum and improve morale of our employees.

Celebration overview


Celebrations at work place brings all individuals closer and provide opportunity to interact across cultures and traditions. We, at Exemed Pharmaceuticals, believe that celebrations induce a strong sense of belonging within employees, as they feel like an integral part of organizations extended family.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi, also known as Vinayaka Chaturthi is a prominent Hindu festival, celebrated across the country with great devotion. The day marks the birth of Lord Ganesha, who is a symbol of wisdom, prosperity and good fortune. Besides praying for blessings and prosperity, we at Exemed take this opportunity to build an enthusiastic spirit of working with employees. The celebration helps to bring the staff together and boost their morale. This improves team working with a fresh mindset and keeps the office environment happy and spiritual.

Town hall meetings

These are often the centerpiece of a company’s internal communication plan. They are leadership’s annual, quarterly or, sometimes more frequent, opportunity to talk with employees. At Exemed, Town hall meetings acts as a tool to convey the business results, priorities, future plans and strategies, and to recognize individual or team achievements.

Republic day

Decorating the office and cubicles, hoisting the tricolour flag, organising a quiz and Rangoli contest almost always fills the employees with enthusiasm. Decorating the office with colourful balloons, ribbons, rangolis, and other decorative elements, focusing on the tricolour of the Indian flag, would certainly swell things up with pride back at our office.

Navratri and dusshera

It’s the celebration of good over evil, of being fearless, and epitomises determination and dedication, strength and courage in the avatars of Lord Durga. At Exemed, we organize a special Graba event with traditional Dandiya Ras to get a chance to see the dancing skills of our employees.

Diwali Celebration

Diwali, a beautiful festival filled with lights, laughter, love, and positivity, is not only a festival but an emotion. A time that brings people closer, wherein colleagues become friends and friends become family. It’s an occasion of showering the wishes of “Happy Diwali ” and exchanging sweets with each other. Employees exchanging gifts helps boost the feelings of fellowship within the organization.

Women’s day

To recognize and honor the contributions of all women in the workplace or outside the workplace, International Women's Day is celebrated each year. It is a global observance that celebrates women's social, economic, cultural, and political achievements. At Exemed, we celebrate this day to appreciate, reward and recognize the female employees for their hard work. The celebration includes various cultural activities, games and gifts followed by a lunch.

Foundation day

Foundation day of the company is an essential milestone in past and future of the company and this is the cause of celebration. This day is the moment to remember the past, study present and enlighten future. It is also a chance to appreciate every pillar in the form of the employee who has stood the test of the time of hard times and given best to lead the company to its success.

Corporate social responsibility

CSR takes many different shapes and forms but is ultimately about companies contributing positively to society and their stakeholders. is the idea that companies should invest positively in their workforce, community, and world. We undertake activities related to social causes and development, environment, disaster relief and community support.

Safety Week

Safety week is celebrated to ensure that safety and health are integrated in work culture and life style. It also helps to renew the commitment of the employees towards safety and health at workplace. It starts with the administration of Safety & Health Pledge by the employees. Various activities like safety slogan and poster competition, demonstrations of firefighting and PPEs, training programs are conducted during this week.

Uttarayan celebration

Celebrating festivals at office is nice concept to bring people close and enhance culture of the organisation. A day of flying kites, sweets and having a good time with your loved ones. We organize “Kite-flying” celebration. This is a festival of colorful kites, Authentic Gujarati Cuisines and lots of happiness.
