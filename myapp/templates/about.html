Mr.Pranav Patel

Managing Director

Exemed Pharmaceuticals

Mr. Pranav Patel is Founder and Executive director of Oneiro Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. He also currently superheads Exemed Pharmaceuticals. Mr. Patel holds a Master's in Business Management with a specialization in Finance from the University of Illinois at Chicago. He returned to India to join his family-owned business enterprise in 2004. After sharpening his saw in manufacturing for a range of pharmaceutical ingredients that are used as raw materials in the pharmaceutical industry, he was able to blend entrepreneurial spirit and technical expertise. In 2009, Mr. Patel started the State of art manufacturing facility of Metformin API to serve regulated international markets like US & Europe. Exemed Pharmaceuticals, today, is a name to reckon with in the highly competitive Pharmaceuticals industry as a trusted supplier of high-quality APIs. He is also a managing committee member of Federation of Gujarat Industry and vadodara Cricket Association.

About Exemed Pharmaceuticals Company

Since

2009

Exemed is a regulatory-compliant, customer-centric, and global leading pharmaceutical company in India. It was established in 2009 as part of a venture initiated by Mr. Pranav Patel. It is supported by two manufacturing facilities involved in the manufacturing of Drug-intermediates and specialty chemicals. Our state-of-the-art US FDA, EDQM, EU GMP certified facility is located in Luna and Vapi and headquartered in Vadodara, Gujarat.
